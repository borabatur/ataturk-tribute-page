Mustafa Kemal Atatürk

The founder and first president of the Turkish Republic

Here's a time line of Mustafa Kemal Atatürk's life:

  • 1881 - Mustafa Kemal was born in Thessaloniki.
  • 1888 - Mustafa, who started his education life, first went to Hafız Mehmed Efendi Neighborhood School and then to Şemsi Efendi School and finished his primary school here.
  • 1893 - Mustafa Kemal took the exams of Thessaloniki Military High School, secretly from his mother, and by passing these exams, he started to receive secondary school education in this school.
  • 1896 - He entered the Manastır Military High School for his high school education.
  • 1899 - Mustafa Kemal, who left Thessaloniki and came to Istanbul, entered the Istanbul Military Academy and graduated from there with the rank of lieutenant.
  • 1902 - After the Military College, he studied at the Military Academy and graduated from there in 1905 as a staff captain.
  • 1905 - He started his duty in the 5th Army, headquartered in Damascus.
  • 1906 - Mustafa Kemal, together with his friends, founded the "Vatan ve Hürriyet Cemiyeti" in Damascus.
  • 1909 - He was appointed as the Chief of Staff of the Thessaloniki Redif Division, which was attached to the 3rd Army. In the same year, the 31 March Incident happened in Istanbul. The Action Army under the command of Mahmut Şevket Pasha started to march from Thessaloniki to Istanbul. Mustafa Kemal was the chief of staff of this army.
  • 1911 - He was assigned to the General Staff Headquarters in Istanbul. Participating in the Tripoli War, he fought against the Italian invaders especially in Tobruk and Derne. Mustafa Kemal became a major while he was in Tripoli.
  • 1912 - Upon the outbreak of the Balkan War, he was appointed as the director of the operations branch of the corps established in Bolayır.
  • 1913 - He ensured that Edirne was taken back from the Bulgarians in the 2nd Balkan War.
  • 1914 - He was promoted to lieutenant colonel.
  • 1915 - He was very effective in winning the Çanakkale Wars.
  • 1916 - He was promoted to brigadier general. He went to Diyarbakir with the 16th Corps, of which he was appointed Commander. Fought on the Caucasian Front against the Russians
  • 1917 - He was appointed to the 7th Army Command under the command of the Yıldırım Army Group. He fought against the British in the First World War Palestine Front.
  • 1918 - After the signing of the Armistice of Mudros, he returned to Istanbul.
  • 1919 - He was appointed as 9th Army Inspector and landed in Samsun on 19 May 1919. He started the War of Independence by issuing the Havza circular. Atatürk determined the purpose and administration of the Turkish War of Independence with the Amasya Circular, Erzurum Congress and Sivas Congresses.
  • 1920 - The Turkish Grand National Assembly was opened.
  • 1921 - He won the Battles of 1st and 2nd İnönü and Sakarya against the Greek occupation forces. The Turkish army entered Izmir and Izmir was liberated from the Greek occupation.
  • 1922 - The Great Offensive, which started on August 26, ended on August 30 with the decisive defeat of the Greeks.
  • 1923 - The Lausanne Peace Treaty was signed.
  • 1923 - In 1923, under the leadership of Atatürk, the Grand National Assembly of Türkiye proclaimed the Republic.
  • 1923-1938 - After the proclamation of the Republic of Türkiye, a period of great restoration began. A series of reforms were carried out in more than one field, including education, law, economic and social life, and great efforts were made to raise this new republic to the level of contemporary civilizations. With these revolutions, the Republic of Turkey has become one of the most respected states in the world.
  • 1938 - On the morning of 10 November 1938, Mustafa Kemal Atatürk died.
